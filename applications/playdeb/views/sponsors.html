What is a sponsor?

An individual, a project, a website or a company who sets up a recurring donation to help PlayDeb financially on a montly basis.

How much does it costs?

The amount is decided by the sponsor. The more the sponsor gives the better but the important thing is to give something.

What are the advantages of this system for PlayDeb?

By sponsoring PlayDeb you contribute to giving the development team a regular and predictable income. This is the best form of income for PlayDeb and for its developers/packagers to start working full time on it. We want PlayDeb to finance itself and are targeting for 200 € per month to have servers paid and to upgrade the amount of bandwidth for the servers.

What are the advantages of this system for the sponsors?

Sponsors get their name associated with the success of PlayDeb. If they have a logo we show it on the front page with a link to their website (each sponsor's chances of being shown is equal to the percentage his contribution represents among other sponsors)
